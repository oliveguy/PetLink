Your pet matching app for socializing pets! Here's a quick tutorial on how to use the app:

First, create a profile

Next, search for matches:

Use the search function to find other pets that match your pet's breed, age, and location

Chat and arrange playdates:

Once you've found a match, start chatting with the owner to arrange a playdate for your pets.

Check out the calendar of pet-friendly events in your area, and attend events to meet other pet owners and their furry friends.
